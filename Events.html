Aeromodelling Workshop

 10-03-2018

Aeromodelling is an event for people from all branches and fields. It teaches the participants the basics of aero design. RC aircraft is a unit rotor air vehicle which has marked it's interest to the benefits of both civil and military domains. Our workshop will help participants to understand the concept and use of accelerometer sensor, microcontroller and wireless communication in designing and controlling a RC aircraft. Then we let them create their own planes using a kit that we provide to every group of participants. Our Aero design experts from other National teams help them learn how to make a basic plane that will fly the farthest. We leave the smaller adjustments up to them. The main motive behind this workshop is to provide practical knowledge to students. Then we do a small competition and see whose plane flies the farthest.
