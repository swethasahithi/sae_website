About Us

SAE (Society of Automotive engineers) international is a global alliance of engineers and technically pundits in the aerospace, automotive and commercial vehicle industries. Its core competencies are life-long, deep-rooted erudition, nurturing and standards development. SAE-VIT is the collegiate club which is a part of SAE INDIA. The chapter’s endeavor is to impart the golden knowledge to its members in the rocket-fast technologies and advancements in the field and pioneer them to the worldwide arena. It is a platform to reach your pinnacle in the quest for advanced technical wisdom topped with a mesmerizing experience of a much desired future and opportunities galore.
